OBiBa is an international project whose mission is to build open source software for epidemiological studies and biobanks. Obiba software consists of an integrated suite of software applications that supports end-to-end epidemiological data cycle life, from its collection and management to its analysis and dissemination. Our software can be used both by individual studies or a networks of studies that want to share their data. Our software is developed in close partnership with large-scale studies and consortia such as the Canadian Longitudinal Study on Aging (CLSA), the Canadian Partnership for Tomorrow (CPT) and BioSHARE.
